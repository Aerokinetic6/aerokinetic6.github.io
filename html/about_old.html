Psychedelic and metal fusion

Most songs we are composing, want to summon dreamlike atmospheres. They intend to bring the undescribable dimensions of moods and visions that intense or even lucid dreams make possible to access.
​

Our mission of creating music is driven by the constant search for answers to ageless questions such as what is the ultimate truth, who we are, what is conciousness, or do we exist at all?
We have a strong desire to comprehend the vast complexity and ambivalent nature of our reality in an open way. Dreams may play a defining role in the future to understand ourselves and the world we are living in, but even if not, they will always be a great source of inspirations, and our magical realm to get lost and explore endlessly by any form of human art.

To reach our above mentioned goal we have chosen a path only very few has taken:
The fine tuned mixture of ambient and metal tones. These two genres are the core components of our songs, but with time we are determined to constantly improve and experiement other styles of music to enrich and diversify our work in a balanced way.
